Gulab jamun

After a spicy and flavorful chicken biryani you need to have a Gulab jamun

Gulab Jamun is a favourite Indian sweet for most Indians. Gulab Jamun recipe is your gateway to end any lavish meal

Time taken to cook the dish is 30 mins. Gulab Jamun recipe is easy enough for beginners to learn how to prepare it in their first try.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tin nestle milkmaid
  • 200gm all purpose flour
  • 600gm grated paneer
  • 200gm semolina
  • 11/2 tbsp baking powder
  • 11/2 tbsp baking soda
  • sunflower oil
  • 2 litres water
  • 1kg sugar
  • 6-8 cardamoms

Steps

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire; add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, nestle MILKMAID , baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough, do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  4. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup, once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame.
  5. Serve warm
