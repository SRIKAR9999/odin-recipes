Mango lassi

Taking too long to make gulab jamun after eating a spicy biryani try mango lassi

Mango lassi is a delicious creamy drink with mango, yogurt, milk, a little sugar, and a sprinkling of cardamom. It's cool and refreshing on a hot day!

Time taken to cook the dish is 10 mins. It serves two people.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped very ripe mango
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 teaspoons honey or sugar, more or less to taste
  • Dash ground cardamom
  • Ice, optional

Steps

  1. Put the mango, yogurt, milk, honey (or sugar), and cardamom into a blender and blend for 2 minutes.
  2. Pour contents into a glass and sprinkle with a tiny pinch of ground cardamom to serve.
